Our Mission

Combat food waste and support communities by connecting surplus food to those in need.

What We Do

  • Facilitate Donations: Provide an easy-to-use platform for donating surplus food through our SharePlate App. Enable real-time updates and location-based notifications to connect donors and recipients efficiently.

  • Reduce Waste: Minimize environmental impact by redistributing surplus food to those in need. Combat food waste with innovative solutions like our SharePlate Van.

  • Support Communities: Help individuals and organizations access fresh, nutritious food. Foster collaboration with shelters, NGOs, and food banks to ensure food reaches the right hands.

  • The SharePlate Van – Our Unique Advantage:

    • Vacuum Sealing Counter: Maintains food freshness for extended periods, ensuring safe delivery even over long distances.
    • Portable Heater: Keeps meals warm and ready to serve, meeting health and safety standards.
    • Thermal Bags: High-quality bags ensure that food quality remains intact during transport.
    • Efficient Logistics: The van is designed for quick pickups and deliveries, ensuring timely redistribution.
    Together, the SharePlate App and Van make food redistribution seamless, sustainable, and impactful. Join us in building a future where no meal is wasted, and everyone has access to quality food.

Our Vision

A world where no good food goes to waste and everyone has access to the food they need.

Join Us

Help us reduce food waste and support your community. Together, we can make a difference.

Why Choose Share Plate?

Choose Share Plate to make a real difference in reducing food waste and supporting your community. Our app offers a user-friendly interface for easy food donations, real-time listings to keep you informed, and secure location handling to protect your privacy. Join us in creating a sustainable future where no food goes to waste, and everyone has access to nutritious meals. Be part of the solution with Share Plate.
